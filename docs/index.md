# Welcome to HomeClaw

**HomeClaw** is a **local-first AI assistant** that runs on your own hardware. Talk to it over the channels you already use—Telegram, Discord, WebChat, email, and more—and extend it with plugins and skills. Your data stays yours.

## One sentence

HomeClaw is **local- and cloud-LLM supported**, **multi-user**, **memory-based**, and **self-hosted**. One agent, one memory, many channels—your home, your AI, your control.
