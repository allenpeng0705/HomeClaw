Privacy Policy for Veda Notes

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

At Veda Notes, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Data Storage

All your journal entries, photos, and other data are stored locally on your device. We do not store any of your data on our servers or in the cloud by default.

  • Your data remains on your device at all times
  • No data is automatically synced to cloud services
  • You have complete control over your data

2. AI Processing

All AI processing happens locally on your device. We do not send your data to external servers for AI analysis.

  • AI models run entirely on your device
  • No data is transmitted for AI processing
  • Your privacy is maintained even when using AI features

3. Backups and Exports

When you create backups or export your data, these files are stored locally on your device. You can choose to share these files with cloud services if you wish, but we do not automatically upload them.

  • Backups are stored locally by default
  • You control where to store your exported data
  • Exports are encrypted for security

4. Third-Party Services

Veda Notes does not integrate with third-party services by default. If you choose to use third-party services (such as cloud storage), any data you share with those services is subject to their privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Local storage encryption
  • Secure backup encryption
  • Protected AI model storage

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual entries can be deleted within the app
  • All data can be cleared from the settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

7. Children's Privacy

Veda Notes is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at shilei.peng@qq.com.

10. Your Consent

By using Veda Notes, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
