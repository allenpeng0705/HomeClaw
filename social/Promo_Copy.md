## English

### Short (one-liner)

**HomeClaw — Your home. Your AI. Your control.** A local-first AI assistant that runs on your machine and talks to you over Telegram, Discord, WebChat, and more. Sibling to the OpenClaw vision: your data stays yours.  
👉 https://github.com/allenpeng0705/HomeClaw

### Medium (2–3 sentences)

**HomeClaw** is a **local-first AI assistant** that runs on your own hardware. Talk to it over the channels you already use—Telegram, Discord, WebChat, email—and extend it with plugins and skills. Same spirit as OpenClaw: your home, your data, your control. Fully open source.  
🔗 https://github.com/allenpeng0705/HomeClaw

### Long (paragraph)

**HomeClaw** is a local-first AI assistant for your home. It runs on your machine and talks to you over Telegram, Discord, Slack, WebChat, email, and more—one agent, one memory, one set of tools and plugins. You can use only local models (llama.cpp, GGUF) or mix in cloud (OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek). Like OpenClaw, it’s built for people who want AI that stays on their side: decentralized, private when you want it, and fully under your control. Open source, Apache 2.0.  
**Star and try it:** https://github.com/allenpeng0705/HomeClaw

### Bullet points (for posts or README)

- **Local-first** — Runs on your machine; use local LLMs so data stays at home.
- **Channel-agnostic** — Same assistant on Telegram, Discord, WebChat, email, and more.
- **Plugins & skills** — Extend with weather, news, mail, or custom workflows.
- **Sibling to OpenClaw** — Same vision: your home, your AI, your control.
- **Open source** — Apache 2.0 | https://github.com/allenpeng0705/HomeClaw
